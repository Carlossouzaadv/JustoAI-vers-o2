## 3. Sales & Revenue Model

### 3.1 Sales Process

```
Stage 1: Awareness → Consideration (1 week)
├─ Inbound: Blog, organic search
├─ Outbound: Cold email + Linkedin
├─ Timing: User realizes pain point
└─ Action: Visits website, clicks "See Demo"

Stage 2: Evaluation (1–2 weeks)
├─ Watch 2-min product video
├─ Try free trial (30 days, 100 credits)
├─ Talk to sales (optional discovery call)
└─ Experience: Try on own case

Stage 3: Trials & Proof (1 week)
├─ Upload real case document
├─ See instant analysis + JUDIT data
├─ Share with team (if multi-user)
├─ ROI calculator: "You'll save R$X/year"

Stage 4: Decision (3–7 days)
├─ Pick plan: Starter, Professional, Enterprise
├─ Sign contract
├─ Activate subscription (Stripe)
└─ Onboarding call (30 min)

Timeline: 2–4 weeks total
```

### 8.3 CAC vs LTV

```
Typical Firm (Professional Plan):
├─ Price: R$699/mês
├─ Lifetime: 24 months (2 years, typical)
├─ Gross margin: 85%
├─ Lifetime revenue: R$699 × 24 × 0.85 = R$14.256

CAC Calculation:
├─ Total sales budget: R$152K
├─ Customers acquired: 50–100
├─ CAC: R$1.520–3.040 per customer

CAC Payback:
├─ Monthly profit per customer: R$699 × 0.85 = R$594
├─ Payback = CAC / monthly profit
├─ Payback = R$2.000 / R$594 = 3.4 months

LTV/CAC Ratio:
├─ LTV/CAC = R$14.256 / R$2.000 = 7:1
├─ Healthy SaaS: >3:1
├─ Our target: >10:1 ✅
```
